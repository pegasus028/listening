How to find your Line ID

Your Line ID is a short username you chose (not your display name). Here's how to find it:

  • 1Open the LINE app on your phone.
  • 2Tap the Home tab (the house icon, bottom-left).
  • 3Tap the Settings gear icon (top-right).
  • 4Tap Profile.
  • 5Find ID in the list — that is your Line ID.

If it shows "Not set", you haven't made one yet. Tap it to create a unique ID (note: once set, it can't be changed).
